About the Theatre

MSU's campus community was sad to see the historic Venue theatre close in 2009. Adjacent to The Coffee Tree Cafe, the theatre provided entertainment for the campus area for decades.

In 2011, Michel jumped at an opportunity, and purchased the adjacent theatre with plans to add on to the Cafe. After months of renovation, we are excited to announce the opening of the theatre space. It will allow live entertainment for our guests!
