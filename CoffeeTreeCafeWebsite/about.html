OUR VISION

The Coffee Tree Cafe seeks to serve campus town residents and visitors premium food and drinks, and provide live entertainment during the day and at night.

OUR TEAM

We are at your service! You'll find Michel and Sandy serving food and drinks, and chatting with the guests. Additionally, our cafe is staffed with some of the hardest working students on campus! Please let us know how we can serve you! Our goal is to exceed your expectations, and hopefully see you regularly!

HOW WE STARTED

A shared love of good coffee and school pride brought Michel Bohmer and Sandy Thomas together while they were graduate students at MSU. Together, they founded The Coffee Tree Cafe in 2006, with a distinct vision to provide a funky destination for students and locals to enjoy drinks, food, and entertainment.
